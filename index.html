About Me

I have over 18 years experience in UI/UX design, information architecture, prototyping, front-end web design and development. I‘m passionate about all things web and tech.

UI / UX / Unite

For me design is not just about making things look “pretty”, it's a beautiful marriage of aesthetics, functionality and usability. I design so users can get from A to B without having to think too hard. No information overload. No confusing grouping of information. No over complex journeys.

It’s a Science

My knowledge in cognitive science has allowed me to form a greater understanding of the human thought process, how people take in information, process that information and in turn interact within their environment. Understanding this branch of psychology has been the key for me in designing the most user centric interfaces and providing an exceptional user experience.

UI Mock ups and Axure Prototype - Home Automation App

Click here to view the interactive prototype
Believe it or not, the most difficult part of the Axure prototype was creating the rotation on the temperature dial. Anyone who uses Axure will know that there is no predefined component for rotation in Axure, the final result was achieved by using a combination of CSS3 animation affects and JavaScript triggers. Admittedly I had to hit the forums for help with this one :)
